<p><p>Industrial Direct Laser Writing Systems are categorized primarily into Desktop and Vertical types. They are ideal for research and small-scale manufacturing. In contrast, Vertical systems are often larger and optimized for high-throughput applications, supporting larger substrates and more extensive production needs. These systems are suited for industries requiring rapid and precise laser processing, such as electronics and microfabrication.</p></p>
