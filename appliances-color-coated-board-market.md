<p><p>The Appliances Color Coated Board market focuses on specialized boards used in manufacturing appliances, with two main types: PCM (Pre-Coated Metal) and VCM (Vinyl Coated Metal). PCM involves a metal substrate that is coated with a colorful, protective layer, offering aesthetic appeal and durability. In contrast, VCM utilizes a vinyl film that is bonded to the metal surface, providing additional flexibility in design and enhanced resistance to environmental factors. Both types cater to the growing demand for visually appealing and long-lasting appliance exteriors.</p></p>
