<p><strong>The Sign Sheeting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass bead type</li><li>Micro prismatic type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sign sheeting markets are primarily categorized into two types: glass bead and micro-prismatic sheeting. Glass bead sheeting consists of a layer of glass beads encapsulated within a polymer, enhancing reflectivity, making it suitable for standard road signs. In contrast, micro-prismatic sheeting employs advanced micro-prism technology, providing superior brightness and visibility, particularly in low-light conditions. This makes micro-prismatic sheets ideal for high-traffic and complex signage scenarios, offering enhanced safety and effectiveness in conveying information to drivers.</p></p>
