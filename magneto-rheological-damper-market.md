<p><p>The magneto-rheological damper market comprises two main types: active dampers and semi-active dampers. Active dampers utilize an external power source to control the damping force, allowing for precise adjustments based on real-time conditions, which enhances performance in dynamic environments. In contrast, semi-active dampers adjust their properties using the magnetic field of the magneto-rheological fluid, allowing for a balance of responsiveness and energy efficiency. Both types improve riding comfort and vehicle stability across various applications, from automotive to industrial systems.</p></p>
