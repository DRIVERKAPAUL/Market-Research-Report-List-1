<p><ul><li>LSZH</li><li>FRNC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The halogen-free control cable market primarily includes two types: Low Smoke Zero Halogen (LSZH) and Flame Retardant Non-Corrosive (FRNC) cables. LSZH cables emit minimal smoke and no halogen gases when exposed to fire, making them ideal for confined spaces, ensuring better visibility and lower toxicity during evacuations. FRNC cables offer similar benefits but with additional flame-retardant properties, limiting fire spread and preventing corrosive substances, which is essential in industrial and commercial applications to enhance safety and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
