<p><p>The diabetic foot ulcer therapy product market is segmented into three primary applications: neuropathic ulcers, ischemic ulcers, and neuro-ischemic ulcers. Neuropathic ulcers stem from nerve damage and lack sensation, often leading to unnoticed injuries. Ischemic ulcers occur due to inadequate blood supply, resulting in tissue damage. Neuro-ischemic ulcers are a combination of both, involving nerve damage and compromised blood circulation. Each ulcer type requires specialized treatment solutions, driving demand for innovative products that promote healing and prevent complications in diabetic patients.</p></p>
