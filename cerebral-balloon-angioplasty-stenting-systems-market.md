<p><p>The Cerebral Balloon Angioplasty and Stenting Systems market comprises two main types: Carotid Artery Stents and Embolic Protection Systems. Carotid Artery Stents are designed to treat stenosis in the carotid arteries, improving blood flow to the brain and reducing stroke risk. Embolic Protection Systems are used during angioplasty procedures to capture potential debris that may dislodge and cause embolic events, thereby enhancing patient safety. Together, these systems play a crucial role in addressing cerebrovascular diseases.</p></p>
