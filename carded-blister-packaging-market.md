<p><ul><li>Thermoforming</li><li>Cold forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carded blister packaging market comprises two primary types: thermoforming and cold forming. Thermoforming involves heating a plastic sheet to mold it over a product and securing it with a backing card, providing visibility and protection. Cold forming uses a foil material that is shaped at room temperature, offering superior barrier properties and tamper resistance. Both methods are widely used for pharmaceuticals and consumer goods, enhancing product appeal while ensuring safety and longevity.</p></p>
