<p><p>The manufacturing industrial casters market comprises two primary types: swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters feature a rotating fork that allows for easy maneuverability in multiple directions, making them ideal for dynamic environments like warehouses and hospitals. In contrast, rigid casters have a fixed wheel alignment that provides straight-line stability, making them suitable for transporting heavy loads over longer distances. Each type serves distinct operational needs, enhancing efficiency and mobility in various industrial applications.</p></p>
