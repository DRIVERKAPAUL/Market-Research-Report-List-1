<p><strong>The Actinic Keratosis Therapeutics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Topical Medications</li><li>Photodynamic Therapy</li><li>Destructive Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Actinic Keratosis therapeutics market comprises three primary treatment types. Topical medications, such as 5-fluorouracil and imiquimod, work by targeting and destroying abnormal skin cells. Photodynamic therapy involves the application of photosensitizing agents followed by light exposure to eliminate affected tissues. Destructive treatments encompass procedures like cryotherapy, electrodessication, and laser therapy, which physically remove or destroy lesions. Each method offers distinct benefits and varies in application based on lesion size, location, and patient preference.</p></p>
