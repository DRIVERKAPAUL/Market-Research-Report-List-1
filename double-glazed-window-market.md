<p><p>The double-glazed window market features various glass types, each offering unique benefits. Float glass is standard and provides clarity and stability. Toughened glass is heat-treated for increased strength and safety, ideal for high-impact areas. Coated glass has a special surface treatment to enhance energy efficiency or reduce glare. Low-E glass is coated with a microscopically thin layer that reflects heat while allowing light, making it highly efficient for insulation and temperature control in buildings.</p></p>
