<p><p>The special animal feed market comprises three main types: compound feed, fodder, and forage. Compound feed refers to manufactured mixtures of ingredients tailored for specific livestock needs, providing balanced nutrition. Fodder encompasses various feed crops cultivated for animal consumption, often as a supplement to other feeds. Forage includes plant materials, particularly grasses and legumes, that are grazed or harvested for feeding, ensuring livestock have access to natural, nutrient-rich food sources, promoting health and growth in animals.</p></p>
