<p><ul><li>Bracket Type</li><li>Positive Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Volume Hood market includes two primary types: Bracket Type and Positive Displacement. Bracket Type hoods are mounted on a support, allowing for easy positioning over air sources to measure airflow accurately. These are commonly used in HVAC systems for routine air quality assessments. Positive Displacement hoods, on the other hand, utilize a mechanism to capture and contain air flow directly, ensuring precise measurement even in high-velocity conditions. Both types are essential for effective air management in various settings.</p></p>
